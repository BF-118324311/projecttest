Summer Camp Volunteer

Edmund Rice Summer Camps

July 2018

I volunteered for this camp during summer.
This camp provides a fun and friendly atmosphere
for kids who come from family who are going through
a rough patch in life, from being poor to
having an extremely rough time in school, they are more
than welcome to come to this free Summer Camp.
I was assigned a kid each day and had to make sure
his/her needs were met throughout the day and that
he/she was having a good time.
