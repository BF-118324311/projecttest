Portugal

I first went to Portugal when I was 5 years old.
We visited Opporto, Lisbon and Algarve.
The weather there is rerally nice. It is always sunny and warm.
I loved the food there.
I have visited Portugal at least 6 times since then.
I have swam with dolphins and done lots of shopping in Portugal.
Everytime I go, I get to have a whole new experience and I love it.

Tenerife

I went to Tenerife 3 years ago.
I absolutely loved going to the beach there.
The sand in Tenerife is black.
Walking in Tenerife is so relaxing.
It is the perfect place for relaxation and I would completely recommend it.

France

I went to Paris, France in 2016. We visited Disneyland while we were there.
I went up the Eiffel Tower too. The experience was unforgettable.
I could see all of Paris from the top. The food in Frnace is really good too.
I loved listening to the language. I learned a lot of french while I was there.

Poland

I visited Poland for the first time last February. We went to Krakow.
It had been snowing for the last few weeks before we went so everywhere we went was full of snow.
I tried skiing with my dad. It was great fun. We spent the whole day on the mountain trying to perfect our skiing method.
In the other days we spent our time making snowmen and sliding down snow hills.
There was a really good pizza place near where we were staying and we went to it every second day.
It was a great trip.
